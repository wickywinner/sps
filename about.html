About & Achievements

Saharsa Public School, Saharsa is an unaided School for boys & girls. estiblished and administered by the "Maa Sushila foundation". Saharsa Public School, Saharsa established in 1980 is affiliated to be C.B.S.E in future, Its old name was SISHU SIKSHA NIKETAN and renamed as Saharsa Public School in 1997. It opened in 1980 in Kayastola Saharsa. Saharsa Public School, Saharsa offers its student not only Professional qualification but also a holistic human education, the possibility of building up their life based on human principles of justice and euqality.It aims at the integral and social growth of the students, by bringing them to encounter with their cultural humanistic and technical heritage. The aim of Saharsa Public School, Saharsa is to impart sound value based education by forming in the student's habits of piety, virtue, Discipline and self-reliance during the years of their studies.
